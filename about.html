About EduPlay

EduPlay is a small collection of free browser games built to help learners practice everyday school skills, like multiplication facts, spelling, or geography, through short, repeatable rounds instead of worksheets.

Why we built it

Repetition is how most people get fast and confident at fundamentals like times tables. A timer and a score turn that repetition into something closer to a game than a chore, which makes it easier to keep coming back to.

Who it's for

Anyone who wants quick, no-signup practice: students, parents helping with homework, teachers looking for a five-minute warm-up activity, or adults brushing up on skills they haven't used in a while.

No accounts, no downloads

Every game runs directly in your browser. There's nothing to install and nothing to sign up for; you press play and start practicing.

Have an idea for a game you'd like to see added? We're actively building out more subjects, listed on the home page.
